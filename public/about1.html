About Ananth N

Ananth N, also known as Ananth Nagarajan, is a passionate full stack developer and AI engineer based in Madurai, Tamil Nadu. Ananth's journey into software development started with curiosity — taking apart websites to understand how they worked, then rebuilding them from scratch. Today, he ships production-grade applications that combine modern frontend frameworks, robust backend systems, and intelligent AI capabilities.

Ananth's approach to development is defined by three pillars: write clean, maintainable code; build experiences that users genuinely enjoy; and leverage AI to solve real problems rather than chasing hype. Every project in his portfolio reflects this philosophy, from interactive 3D portfolios built with Three.js to AI-powered chatbots with retrieval-augmented generation pipelines.

Beyond writing code, Ananth is deeply committed to education. Through the Naan Mudhalvan government initiative and one-on-one mentorship, he has taught over 150 students the fundamentals of web development and AI engineering. This teaching experience has sharpened his ability to communicate complex technical concepts clearly — a skill that directly benefits his clients and collaborators.

My Development Journey

Ananth's development journey began like many great tech stories — with a computer, an internet connection, and an insatiable curiosity. Starting with HTML and CSS, he quickly moved into JavaScript and Python, building small tools and websites that solved everyday problems. The transition from hobbyist to professional happened naturally as projects grew in complexity and scope.

Over time, Ananth mastered the full stack: React and Next.js for frontend, Node.js and Express for backend, and PostgreSQL and MongoDB for data. His fascination with AI led him to explore machine learning, LLM APIs, and vector databases, eventually becoming an AI engineer capable of building production-ready AI systems. The portfolio you see on ANANTH.DEV is the culmination of years of deliberate practice, countless late-night coding sessions, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Today, Ananth works with clients across India — from startups needing MVPs to enterprises requiring custom ERP and CRM solutions. He remains a student of the craft, constantly exploring new technologies and sharing his knowledge with the developer community.

Technical Expertise & Skills

Ananth's technical toolkit spans the entire modern web development landscape. On the frontend, he works extensively with React, Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS, creating interfaces that are both visually stunning and performant. His expertise with Three.js and GSAP animation enables him to build immersive, interactive web experiences that captivate users.

On the backend, Ananth is proficient in Node.js, Express, Python, and FastAPI, building APIs that are fast, secure, and scalable. He works with both SQL and NoSQL databases — PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and Supabase — and knows how to design schemas that perform at scale. For AI, Ananth integrates OpenAI APIs, LangChain, and vector databases to build intelligent features like semantic search, chatbots, and automated workflows.

Beyond code, Ananth brings strong UI/UX design sensibilities, understanding that great software is as much about the user experience as it is about the underlying architecture. He also practices DevOps fundamentals including Docker containerisation, CI/CD pipelines, and Vercel deployment.
