HRM Development Services

Ananth N offers comprehensive HRM (Human Resource Management) software development services for businesses seeking to streamline workforce management, automate HR operations, and build better employee experiences. Based in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India, Ananth develops custom HR management systems that handle the complete employee lifecycle — from recruitment and onboarding to attendance tracking, payroll processing, performance management, and offboarding. Custom HRM software ensures your HR processes align with your company policies, Indian labor law compliance requirements, and organizational culture rather than forcing your team to adapt to generic HR software built for different markets.

Employee Management & Self-Service Portals

A centralized employee database is the foundation of any HRM system. Ananth builds comprehensive employee management modules with a searchable, filterable employee directory containing contact information, documents, skills, and certifications. Employee self-service portals empower staff to update their personal information, view pay slips, request leave, and access company policies without HR intervention. Organizational charts automatically reflect reporting structures and department changes, while document management securely stores contracts, offer letters, and performance reviews with version control and expiry tracking.

Attendance & Time Tracking

Accurate attendance tracking is essential for payroll, compliance, and workforce planning. Ananth develops flexible attendance management systems supporting multiple tracking methods: biometric integration with existing hardware, geo-fenced mobile check-in for field employees, web-based clock-in/clock-out for remote workers, and manual entry with manager approval for exceptions. Shift management features include configurable shift templates, shift swapping with approval workflows, and overtime calculation based on configurable rules. Real-time attendance dashboards give managers visibility into who is present, late, or absent at any moment. Leave management includes configurable leave policies (earned leave, sick leave, casual leave, and India-specific leave types), accrual rules, balance tracking, and multi-level approval workflows.

Payroll Processing & Compliance

Payroll is one of the most critical HR functions, and getting it right requires a system that understands local compliance requirements. Ananth builds payroll modules that handle salary structure management with configurable earnings (basic, HRA, allowances) and deductions (PF, ESI, professional tax, TDS), automatic payroll computation based on attendance and leave data, India-specific statutory compliance including Provident Fund (PF), Employee State Insurance (ESI), Professional Tax, and Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) calculations, and payslip generation with PDF exports and email delivery. Integration with banking portals for salary disbursement and with accounting software for journal entries ensures end-to-end payroll automation.

Performance Management

Driving employee performance requires a structured yet flexible approach. Ananth builds performance management modules supporting goal setting with OKR (Objectives and Key Results) or KPI frameworks, periodic review cycles (quarterly, half-yearly, annual) with configurable review templates, 360-degree feedback collection from peers, subordinates, and managers, and performance rating and calibration with normalization across teams. Development plans link performance gaps to training programs and growth opportunities. Analytics dashboards provide HR leaders with insights into workforce performance trends, high-potential identification, and retention risk indicators.

Recruitment & Onboarding

Bringing new talent into the organization is streamlined with built-in recruitment and onboarding modules. Features include job requisition management with approval workflows, applicant tracking with resume parsing and candidate scoring, interview scheduling with calendar integration and feedback forms, offer letter generation with digital acceptance, and onboarding checklists that guide new hires through account setup, equipment provisioning, and orientation activities. The entire process is visible through recruitment dashboards showing pipeline metrics, time-to-hire, and source effectiveness.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Indian statutory compliances does your HRM software handle?

The HRM software is built with comprehensive support for Indian statutory compliance requirements. This includes Provident Fund (PF) with configurable employee and employer contribution rates, Employee State Insurance (ESI) with threshold-based calculation, Professional Tax based on state-specific slabs, Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) with Section 80C, 80D, and HRA exemption calculations, Labour Welfare Fund (LWF) where applicable, and Bonus Act compliance. The system automatically computes contributions and deductions based on employee details and salary components, generates statutory reports, and produces challans for payment. All compliance configurations are updated to reflect the latest government regulations.

Can employees access the HRM system from their phones?

Yes, every HRM system I build is fully responsive and works seamlessly on mobile devices through the browser. Employees can apply for leave, view payslips, mark attendance, update personal information, access company policies, and submit expense claims from their phones. Managers can approve requests, view team attendance, and access reports on the go. The mobile interface is designed for touch interactions and works reliably even on slower mobile networks. If you need a native mobile app for iOS and Android, I can build that using React Native.

How does the payroll system handle complex salary structures?

The payroll module supports highly configurable salary structures to accommodate the complexity of Indian compensation models. You can define unlimited earnings components (basic, HRA, special allowance, conveyance, medical, LTA, performance bonus, incentives) and deduction components (PF, ESI, PT, TDS, loan repayment, insurance premiums). Each component can have its own calculation formula — fixed amount, percentage of basic, percentage of gross, or custom formulas. The system supports pro-rata calculations for mid-cycle joiners and leavers, arrears processing, and multi-currency salaries for international employees. All computations are fully auditable with detailed payslips showing every earning and deduction.

Can I integrate the HRM with existing payroll or accounting software?

Yes, the HRM system is designed for seamless integration with your existing software stack. I can integrate with payroll software for salary processing, accounting software (Tally, Zoho Books, QuickBooks) for journal entries, biometric devices for attendance data, email systems (Gmail, Outlook) for communication, and banking portals for salary disbursement. Integration can be real-time via API or scheduled batch synchronization. This ensures your HR data flows smoothly to other systems without manual data entry or reconciliation.

How do you handle data security and employee privacy?

Data security and employee privacy are fundamental to every HRM system I build. Technically, this includes role-based access control ensuring only authorized personnel can view sensitive data, encryption at rest and in transit for all personal and financial information, comprehensive audit logging tracking every data access and modification, session management with automatic timeout, and GDPR-inspired data principles including right to access and deletion. Organizationally, I follow secure development practices and provide documentation for your team to establish proper data handling policies. The system can be deployed on your own infrastructure if required for maximum data control.
