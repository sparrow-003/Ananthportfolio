SaaS Platform Development

SaaS development is one of my core specialities. I build multi-tenant SaaS platforms with user authentication, role-based access control, subscription management, payment gateway integration, and scalable cloud infrastructure. Whether you are launching a new SaaS product or migrating an existing application to a subscription model, I provide end-to-end SaaS development expertise.

My SaaS platforms are built with modern technologies like React, Next.js, TypeScript, Node.js, and PostgreSQL, deployed on Vercel for optimal performance. I implement features like usage analytics, billing dashboards, team management, and API access. Every SaaS platform I build is designed for scalability from day one.

AI & Agent Development

AI agent development represents the frontier of business software. I build intelligent agents that automate customer support, qualify leads, extract data from documents, generate reports, and orchestrate complex business processes. Using OpenAI APIs, LangChain, Anthropic Claude, and vector databases like pgvector, I create AI systems that deliver real, measurable value.

Examples of AI solutions I have built include RAG-powered chatbots that answer questions from your knowledge base, AI agents that automate lead qualification and follow-up, document processing pipelines that extract structured data from invoices and forms, and intelligent dashboards that provide natural language querying of your business data.

CRM & ERP Solutions

CRM development and ERP development are critical for businesses that need to manage customer relationships and operations effectively. I build custom CRM systems that track leads, manage contacts, automate follow-ups, and provide sales analytics — all tailored to your specific sales process. Unlike generic CRMs like Salesforce or HubSpot, a custom CRM fits your workflow perfectly without forcing you to adapt to the software.

For ERP development, I create integrated systems that connect finance, HR, inventory, procurement, sales, and operations into a single platform. Custom ERP solutions eliminate data silos, automate manual processes, and provide real-time visibility into your business performance. I also build specialised systems including HRM development, billing software development, inventory management software, and logistics software development.

Business Process Automation

Automation software transforms how businesses operate. I build custom automation solutions that eliminate repetitive manual tasks, reduce errors, and free up your team to focus on high-value work. From automated invoice processing and email workflows to data synchronisation between systems and scheduled report generation, business software development focused on automation delivers rapid ROI.

API & Database Services

For cloud application development, I deploy applications on Vercel, AWS, or other cloud platforms with CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, and monitoring. Your software is not just built — it is deployed, maintained, and supported for the long term.
