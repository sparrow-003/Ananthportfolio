UI/UX Design Services

As a UI/UX designer based in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, I bridge the gap between design and development. I don't just create beautiful mockups — I build the interfaces that realise them. My design process is research-driven, accessibility-focused, and performance-aware. Whether you need a complete brand experience, a complex enterprise dashboard, or a mobile-first landing page, I deliver designs that are both visually stunning and technically feasible.

UX Research & Strategy

Great design starts with understanding users. I conduct user interviews, competitive analysis, and journey mapping to uncover pain points and opportunities. My research outputs include:

  • User Personas — Data-driven profiles representing your key user segments.
  • Journey Maps — End-to-end visualisations of user interactions and emotions.
  • Information Architecture — Sitemaps and content hierarchies that make navigation intuitive.
  • Competitive Audits — Analysis of competitor UX patterns and design decisions.

Every design decision I make is rooted in this research — never guesswork.

Visual Design & Brand Systems

Typography

I select and scale typefaces with intention — pairing display fonts for impact with readable body typefaces. I establish fluid type scales that work across every viewport size.

Colour Systems

I build accessible colour palettes with sufficient contrast ratios (WCAG AA minimum). My colour systems include semantic tokens for success, warning, error, and info states — ensuring consistency across the entire product.

Layout & Composition

I design responsive layouts using CSS Grid, Flexbox, and container queries. Every design starts mobile-first and scales up to large desktop screens with intentional breakpoints.

Motion & Micro-Interactions

Subtle animations guide attention, provide feedback, and make interfaces feel alive. I design motion that serves a purpose — loading states, hover effects, page transitions, and gesture feedback — using Framer Motion and CSS animations.

Design Systems

I create comprehensive design systems in Figma with component libraries, style dictionaries, and documentation. These systems bridge the gap between design and development, ensuring pixel-perfect implementation with reusable code components.

My Design Process

  1. Discover — Research, stakeholder interviews, and goal definition.
  2. Define — Information architecture, user flows, and content strategy.
  3. Design — Wireframes (low-fi → hi-fi), prototypes, and visual design.
  4. Test — Usability testing, accessibility audits, and iteration.
  5. Deliver — Design handoff with specs, assets, and developer documentation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you design mobile apps or just websites?

Both. I design responsive websites and native mobile app interfaces (iOS and Android). My design process adapts to each platform's conventions while maintaining brand consistency.

Can you implement the designs you create?

Yes — this is my key differentiator. As both a designer and frontend developer, I can implement designs directly in React, Next.js, and Tailwind CSS. This eliminates the handoff gap and ensures the final product matches the vision.

How do you ensure accessibility in your designs?

I design with WCAG 2.1 AA standards from the start — sufficient colour contrast, readable font sizes, clear focus indicators, proper heading hierarchies, and touch-target sizing. I audit designs with axe DevTools and Stark before handoff.

What tools do you use for design?

My primary tool is Figma for wireframing, prototyping, and design systems. I also use Excalidraw for quick ideation, and I hand off designs with detailed specs including spacing tokens, component states, and responsive behaviour notes.

Do you offer design system consulting?

Yes. I help teams build and maintain scalable design systems — from token architecture to component documentation. This includes auditing your current system, planning migration, and establishing governance processes.
