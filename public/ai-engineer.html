LLM Application Development

Ananth develops full-stack LLM applications using OpenAI GPT-4o, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, and open-source models. Applications include streaming chat interfaces, tool-using assistants, function-calling workflows, and structured data extraction. Every integration is built with reliability, latency, and cost monitoring in mind, ensuring your AI application performs well under real-world conditions.

RAG Pipeline Engineering

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) connects LLMs to your proprietary data — documents, databases, knowledge bases, and APIs. Ananth designs end-to-end RAG systems using pgvector, Pinecone, and embedding models, with careful chunking strategies, hybrid search, and re-ranking to ensure the AI retrieves the right context every time. The result is an AI that answers based on your actual content, not generic training data.

AI Agent Development

Ananth builds AI agents that can plan, reason, and execute multi-step workflows autonomously. Using frameworks like LangChain, CrewAI, and custom orchestration, these agents combine LLM reasoning with tool use, memory, and human-in-the-loop validation. Use cases include automated research, customer support triage, data enrichment pipelines, and business process automation.

Prompt Engineering & Optimization

Systematic prompt engineering is the foundation of reliable AI output. Ananth applies structured prompt design, few-shot examples, output formatting, and evaluation frameworks to optimize for accuracy, consistency, and cost. This discipline reduces hallucination, improves response quality, and ensures your AI application meets production standards from day one.
