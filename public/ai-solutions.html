Frequently Asked Questions

What types of AI solutions does Ananth build?

Ananth builds conversational AI chatbots, document intelligence (RAG) systems, content generation pipelines, and analytics automation tools.

How does a custom AI chatbot work?

Custom AI chatbots use LLMs combined with your business data to provide intelligent, context-aware responses. They include memory, tool use, and can be embedded into your website or app.

What is document intelligence with RAG?

Document intelligence uses RAG to let you query your PDFs, manuals, knowledge bases, and other documents using natural language. The AI retrieves relevant content and generates accurate answers.

Can AI automate my business reporting?

Yes, Ananth builds AI-powered analytics automation that ingests data, generates insights, and produces formatted reports — reducing manual analysis time significantly.

How long does it take to build a custom AI solution?

Timelines vary by complexity. A custom chatbot can be deployed in 2-4 weeks, while more complex RAG or automation systems typically take 4-8 weeks for production readiness.
